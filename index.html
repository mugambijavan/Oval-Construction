BRIEF INTRODUCTION

Oval construction company is a company under the Companies Act CAP 486 Laws Act of kenya. It engages in projects in East & Central Africa and is equipped to carry out civil engineering works that pertains Consortium.

We are a one stop center company that enables clients to access services of Architectural , Civil Card, Structural & Bill of quantities. The company works across the public and private sectors.
